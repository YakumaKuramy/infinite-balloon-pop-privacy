# Infinite Balloon Pop - Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 2026_

Mandacaru Game Dev developed the **Infinite Balloon Pop** game as an Ad-Supported app. This service is provided at no cost and is designed for use as is.

### 1. Information Collection and Use

We do not directly collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information from our users. The game does not feature any registration, login, or accounts.

However, the application utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify your device to deliver relevant advertisements.

### 2. Third-Party Services

The game integrates third-party software development kits (SDKs) for advertising purposes:

- **Google Mobile Ads (AdMob):** Used to display banner advertisements. AdMob may collect data such as IP addresses, device identifiers (advertising ID), and interaction logs to serve and personalize ads.

You can read their privacy practices here:

- [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543)

### 3. Local Storage and Game Progress

Game progress data (such as your high score and currency) is saved exclusively local to your own device's internal storage and is deleted if the game is uninstalled.

### 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

### 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at the developer email registered on the Google Play Store.
